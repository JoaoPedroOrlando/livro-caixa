2. Where Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using the `expo-sqlite` database. The app does not transmit any data to external servers, unless you choose to export or share files manually.

3. How We Use the Information

Your data is used solely to:

4. Data Sharing

The app does not automatically share your data with third parties. However, if you use features such as file export or sharing (e.g., `expo-document-picker`, `expo-sharing`), you are responsible for deciding where your data goes.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save and export CSV files.
  • File access: To import data (in future updates, if available).

These permissions are not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

6. Data Security

We follow best practices to protect your data. However, since storage is local to your device, we recommend securing your device with a password and making regular backups if applicable.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can manually erase all app data (by clearing or uninstalling the app)
